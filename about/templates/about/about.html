About Kenneth Matiba Eye and Dental Hospital

Our History & Mission

Kenneth Matiba Eye and Dental Hospital was founded with the mission to provide world-class, compassionate healthcare to all patients, focusing on eye and dental care alongside comprehensive medical services.

  • General Staff: Nurses, Technicians, Support Staff

Core Values & Guiding Principles

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Patient-centered care

Certifications & Affiliations

Accredited by the National Health Authority and affiliated with the International Medical Council.
