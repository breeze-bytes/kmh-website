History & Mission

Kenneth Matiba Hospital has a rich history rooted in a deep commitment to improving healthcare in the region. Established in 2015 in Kenol, the hospital was named in honor of the late Kenneth Matiba, a visionary leader who inspired the values of service, dedication, and excellence.

Initially operating as a specialized Eye and Dental Clinic, the facility quickly earned a reputation for its compassionate care and skilled medical professionals. In 2024, the hospital relocated to Makenji, where it continues to grow and serve a larger community.

Today, Kenneth Matiba Hospital is evolving into a fully-fledged Level 4 Hospital, expanding its services beyond specialized care. New outpatient facilities and emergency structures are currently under development to meet the growing needs of patients and provide comprehensive healthcare services under one roof.

Guided by our mission to deliver the highest quality preventive, promotive, curative, and rehabilitative care, we remain dedicated to ensuring every patient receives compassionate, professional, and accessible healthcare.
