History & Mission

Kenneth Matiba Eye and Dental Hospital was established to provide quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare to the community. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of preventive, promotive, curative, and rehabilitative services, with a special focus on eye and dental care.
