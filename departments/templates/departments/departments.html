KENNETH MATIBA HOSPITAL

Departments & Services

Eye Clinic

Our Eye Clinic offers comprehensive eye examinations, cataract and glaucoma management, diabetic retinopathy screening, pediatric eye care, and surgical procedures such as cataract removal and corneal transplants. We also provide advanced diagnostic imaging and prescription eyewear services.

Dental Clinic

The Dental Clinic provides general dentistry, orthodontics, root canal therapy, oral surgery, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, pediatric dental care, and preventive treatments such as scaling, polishing, and fluoride therapy to maintain optimal oral health.

General Outpatient

We offer general medical consultations, treatment of common illnesses, chronic disease management, wound care, immunizations, minor surgical procedures, and wellness checks to ensure the overall health of our community.

Laboratory

Our fully equipped laboratory provides diagnostic services including blood tests, urinalysis, microbiology, pathology, hematology, and biochemistry. We ensure quick turnaround times and accurate results to support clinical decision-making.

Pharmacy

The Pharmacy offers prescription dispensing, over-the-counter medications, vaccination services, medication counseling, chronic disease medication management, and patient education on safe drug use.

Radiology

Our Radiology Department provides digital X-rays, ultrasound scans, CT scans, and specialized imaging services. We ensure accurate diagnostics with minimal radiation exposure using the latest imaging technology.

Social Work

The Social Work Department offers counseling services, patient advocacy, support for vulnerable individuals, community outreach programs, and assistance in navigating healthcare and social support systems.

Nutrition

Our Nutrition Unit provides dietary assessments, customized meal planning, nutritional counseling for chronic conditions, weight management programs, and therapeutic diets to promote optimal health and recovery.

ICT Department

The ICT Department manages hospital information systems, provides technical support, maintains patient data security, and develops innovative digital solutions to enhance healthcare delivery and hospital operations.
